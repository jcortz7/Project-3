What is Rocket League?

Rocket League is a game of soccer using cars. The point is to hit the ball with the cars and score on your opponents, just like in soccer.

Speed Aerial

Aerialing is an important mechanic because it helps you get to the ball in the air, beating most opponents.

Speed Flip

The Speed Flip is important because being fast helps you get to the ball quicker.

Shooting

Learning to shoot is a very important skill to master early in the game!
